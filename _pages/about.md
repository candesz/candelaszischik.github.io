 I'm a PhD candidate in Physics at Universidad de Buenos Aires, working at Instituto de Fisiología, Biología Molecular y Neurociencias (IFIBYNE UBA-CONICET). My research lies at the interface between physics and biology, with a focus on gene regulatory networks and microRNA-mediated regulation. I have a strong background in nonlinear dynamics and signal processing in biological systems, and experience integrating theoretical modeling with experimental approaches, including molecular biology techniques. I am interested in information processing and decision-making in cellular systems, as well as applying physics-based approaches to problems in the physics of living systems.
